Dutch Baby

A Dutch baby is a big, puffy, family-style pancake baked that often comes covered with wonderful toppings.

A Dutch baby pancake, sometimes called a German pancake, a Bismarck, a Dutch puff, or a Hootenanny, is a large American popover

Buttery and sweet, the plate sized pancake crawls up the side of the pan while cooking, resulting in a bowl-like shape perfect for introducing whatever extra flavors you want. I am particularly fond of powdered sugar and berries, but its even delicious with only cinnamon and butter!

Ingredients:

The ingredients needed to make 3 to 4 dutch babies are:

  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Toppings of your choice!
